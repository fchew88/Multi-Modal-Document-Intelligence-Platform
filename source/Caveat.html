<p>This section allows you to enter the description of land. The compulsory data
items are indicated by an <font color="#CC0000">*</font> marked in <font color="#FF0000">red</font>.</p>
<p><i>To create a record:</i></p>
<table width="100%" border="0" class="normal">
<tr>
<td width="5%" valign="top">
1.
</td>
<td width="95%">
<p><img src="../printscreen/cv/cv_dolSelectTitleType_add.jpg" width="597" height="123"><br>
Select the <b>Title Type [D]</b>. </p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="5%" valign="top">
2.
</td>
<td width="95%">
<p> <img src="../printscreen/cv/cv_dolSSCT_add.jpg" width="597" height="154"><br>
If <b><i>CT</i></b> (Certificate of Title), <b><i>SCT </i></b>(Subsidiary
Certificate of Title) or <i><b>SSCT </b></i>(Subsidiary Strata Certificate
of Title) is selected, enter <b>Vol</b> <b>No [T]</b> and <b>Fol No [T].</b></p>
<p><img src="../printscreen/cv/cv_dolUL_add.jpg" width="596" height="150"><br>
If <b><i>UL</i></b> (Unregistered Land) is selected, <b>Interest in Estate
Affected [T] </b>will be displayed for Caveat (CV). </p>
<p><img src="../printscreen/cv/cv_dolLease_add.jpg" width="597" height="150"><br>
If <i><b>Lease</b></i> is selected, enter the <b>Lease No. [T]</b>.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="5%" valign="top">
3.
</td>
<td width="95%">Select the <i><b>MK</b></i> (Mukim) or <i><b>TS</b></i> (Town
Subdivision) and enter the rest of the <b>Lot No</b>. <b>[D, T]. </b></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="5%" valign="top">
4.
</td>
<td width="95%">Enter <b>Address [S].</b></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="5%" valign="top">
5.
</td>
<td width="95%">Click on <b>Add [B]. </b>Repeat the previous steps to add
more records. </td>
</tr>
</table>
